Four Layers of AI Infrastructure

1. GPU & NVLink

Modern GPUs like A100 and H100 are the foundation for training large AI models. NVLink offers fast interconnect between GPUs, helping reduce bottlenecks in multi-GPU jobs.

2. RDMA Networking

RDMA allows high-speed, low-latency communication across machines — ideal for synchronizing parameters in distributed training.

3. Kubernetes

Kubernetes helps manage and scale AI workloads, schedule jobs, and orchestrate multi-node training or inference pipelines.

4. AI-Native Applications

From training pipelines to inference APIs, the application layer brings models into production and interactive use cases.
